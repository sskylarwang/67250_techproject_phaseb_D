Mission Statement

MonoMuse explores the relationship between art, technology, and society. Our mission is to make contemporary art approachable, interactive, and meaningful for a broad range of visitors through exhibitions, installations, and educational programming.

Who We Serve

MonoMuse welcomes first-time visitors, students, families, artists, researchers, and returning members. We aim to create a space where visitors can engage with digital culture, experimentation, and creative expression.

Milestones

  • 2016: MonoMuse opens with its first digital storytelling exhibition.
  • 2018: Launches interactive media lab for visitors and schools.
  • 2023: Expands gallery space and introduces international collaborations.
